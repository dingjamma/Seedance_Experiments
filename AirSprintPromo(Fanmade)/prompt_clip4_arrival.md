## English Description

The emotional payoff of the whole promo. A family of 4–5 people — mother holding a young daughter's hand, father, and grandparents — descend from the aircraft stairs and walk toward the camera at a tropical airport. Golden hour sunlight hits them from behind, creating warm rim lighting on their silhouettes. Palm trees frame the background. The aircraft (white and navy) is visible behind them with the door open and stairs extended. Camera is low, moving slowly backward as the family walks forward. Slow motion at about 60% speed. The clip ends on a close-up of the little girl smiling. The feeling: this is why you fly privately.
